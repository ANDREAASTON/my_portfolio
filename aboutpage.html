About Me

I am 5th year Electronics and Computer Engineering student at University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS), passionate about designing practical technology that connects hardware, software, and real-world problem-solving. I enjoy building functional robotic systems and digital solutions that improve how people learn, work, and interact with technology.

My experience spans robotics, embedded systems, web development, electrical installation, and computer troubleshooting. I have worked on hands-on robot building projects, website development, and academic engineering tasks — combining creativity with strong technical foundations.

Beyond engineering, I’m also a graphic designer who enjoys producing digital artwork and helping with STEM-focused activities that inspire young innovators.
