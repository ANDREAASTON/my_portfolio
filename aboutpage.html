About Me

I'm a Student Electronics and Computer Engineer at Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS) with a passion for building innovative solutions that bridge the gap between hardware and software. My work focuses on leveraging technology to address real-world challenges, particularly in the African context.

With expertise in robotics, embedded systems, and web development, I create solutions that are both technically robust and user-friendly. My projects often revolve around sustainability, education technology, and digital empowerment initiatives that can make a tangible difference in local communities.

When I'm not coding or tinkering with hardware, you can find me conducting STEM workshops or creating digital art that explores the intersection of technology and African culture.
