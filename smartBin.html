Smart Bin Project

A touchless, sensor-driven waste management system with real-time monitoring and alerts.

Project Overview

The smart bin is an automated waste management solution designed to promote hygiene and efficiency. It uses ultrasonic sensors to detect human presence and garbage level, automatically opens the lid when a person is nearby, and provides visual (LEDs) and audio (buzzer) alerts when the bin is full. This reduces physical contact and improves cleanliness in shared spaces.
