3D Designing

Professional 3D modelling and high-precision resin printing services using SolidWorks, Onshape, and modern SLA printers such as the Photon Mono 2. I have Designed and printed some models for Robotics Foundation Limited. I design durable, detailed, and production-ready components tailored for engineering, prototyping, and functional applications.

Overview

My 3D design and printing workflow combines advanced CAD modelling with high-resolution resin printing to produce accurate and reliable parts. Using industry-standard tools like SolidWorks and cloud-based platforms such as Onshape, I generate fully-optimized models suitable for engineering analysis, mechanical assemblies, or conceptual visualization. Printed using SLA technology, each component delivers exceptional surface quality, tight tolerances, and structural consistency.

Key Capabilities

  • Advanced CAD Modelling: Expertise in SolidWorks and Onshape for mechanical design, assemblies, simulations, and technical drawings.
  • High-Resolution Resin Printing: Using printers like the Photon Mono 2 for smooth, detailed, and dimensionally accurate prints.
  • Functional Prototyping: Creation of strong prototype parts suitable for testing, fitting, and mechanical integration.
  • Design for Manufacture: Optimizing models for printability, strength distribution, support minimization, and post-processing efficiency.
  • Custom Components: Ability to design and print specialized parts for robotics, enclosures, mounts, mechanical assemblies, and hobby projects.
  • Professional Finishing: Post-processing including curing, sanding, priming, and surface smoothing for production-ready results.
