Water Base Flow Robot

An innovative pipe inspection robot designed for navigating water pipes while carrying monitoring equipment.

Project Overview

The Water Base Flow Robot project involved creating a custom, waterproof robot capable of traversing horizontal pipes while carrying a GoPro camera for interior monitoring. The development process spanned digital design in SolidWorks, extensive 3D printing of components, and thorough physical assembly. The project presented significant challenges in waterproofing, structural integrity, and logistical planning for off-site printing. Key achievements included mastering support structure design for complex overhanging parts, implementing dry-fit assembly validation, and learning proper material curing techniques for watertight seals. The robot's internal chassis was successfully assembled, sealed, and tested, with plans for a stronger, single-piece outer shell to enhance overall robustness.

Key Features

  • Pipe Navigation: Designed specifically for horizontal pipe inspection
  • Waterproof Construction: Utilized silicone sealant and strong glue for watertight assembly
  • Camera Integration: Capable of carrying GoPro for visual monitoring
  • Custom 3D Printed Parts: All components designed in SolidWorks and printed locally
  • Dry-Fit Validation: Implemented pre-assembly testing to verify component fit
  • Logistical Management: Coordinated off-site printing with reliable power supply
